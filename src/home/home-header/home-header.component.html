About

Traditional Machine Learning algorithms such as the most powerful Support Vector Machines tend to run very slow on BigData. Neural Nets and Deep Learning not only limited to faster running times, they result in better predictions/classifications and are well suited for all forms of BigData when they are carefully designed.

This channel seek to address NN and DL concepts and help you build your NN and DL models to solve Machine Learning problems of classification, regression and clustering. Implementation on real-life data sets have been considered as well.

We hope you find the resources on this site useful. You can reach us on any of the contact methods provided and we will be ever available to attend to you.
